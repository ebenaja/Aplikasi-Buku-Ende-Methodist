1.	O tondingku bereng Jesus,
    Puji ma nasa gogom
	  Ho ditobus di palua, Puji Raja i tongtong
	  Haleluya, haleluya, Puji Raja i tongtong

2.	Sai endehon ma basaNa, 
    Naung patar ni idami
	  Sai manongtong do asiNa, 
    Tung so olo muba i
	  Haleluya, haleluya, Puji ma basaNa i.

3.	Songon ama do Ibana, Dianahon hita be
	  Diantusi do galenta, Diurupi sasude
	  Haleluya, haleluya, Sai tapuji Debata.

4.	Angka surusuruanNa, 
    Pasangaphon Debata
	  Nasa bangso pe marsomba, 
    Hita pe marende ma
	  Haleluya, haleluya, Tapasangap Debata. 
    Amen
