1.	 Bege ma gokkon ni Jesus 
	   Unang ma tarlalap be,
	   Ai bidang do sisabion,
	   Saluhut nunga rade,
	   Tung gomos gokkon ni Jesus
	   ai balga nang upa i,
	   Ise do na ra maralus,
	   Dokma “au rade tusi”.

2. Ise do na olo borhat,
	   Tu tano na leban i?
	   Tu na dao nang tu na jonok
	   Naeng ma sar barita i,
	   Ise do na mangalehon, 
	   Arta tu ulaon i,
	   Ai Tuhanta paradehon,
	   Upa tu na basa i.

3.	 Unang dok au on na gale,
	   Ulaonku so adong,
	   Naung torop do jolma mate,
	   Mian do di na holom,
	   Rap sude ma hita borhat,
	   Tole ma mangula be,
	   Ganup hita rap mandok ma
	   “ Au dison nunga rade”. Amen.
