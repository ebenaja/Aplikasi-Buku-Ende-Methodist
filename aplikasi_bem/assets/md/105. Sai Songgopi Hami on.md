1.	 Sai songgopi hami on., ale Tondi Parbadia
	   Sai ajari ma tongtong, 
     Hami on huhut paria
	   Martangiang tu Tuhanta,
	   Asa tongtong oloanna.

2. Tondi hasangapon i, sai ingani rohanami
	   Pargogoi ma hami be, 
     Maralohon dosanami
	   Asa tung malua hami,
	   Sian hadosaonnami.

3. Jolma manisia pe, sai sondangi ma lulutna
	  Asa olo sasude, ro tu Jesus, Sipalua
	  Rarat baen harajaonNa.
	  Tu saluhut manisia. Amen.
