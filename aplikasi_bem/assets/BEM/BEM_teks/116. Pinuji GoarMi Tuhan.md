1.	 Pinuji GoarMi Tuhan,
	   Unduk somba tu Ho,
	   Pasangap ma GoarNa i,
	   Sai somba, somba, somba,
	   Somba Tuhan i.

2. Hamu naung tinobusNa i,
	   Mangolu do sude,
	   Pasangap ma Tuhanta i,
	   Sai somba, somba, somba,
	   Somba Tuhan i.

3. Hamu pardosa ingot ma,
	   Jorbut ni uhum i,
	   Tu Debata lehon dirim,
	   Sai somba, somba, somba,
	   Somba Tuhan i.

4.	 O angka bangso puji ma, 
	   Goar ni Tuhan i,
	   Sai somba Jesus Rajami,
	   Pasangap, sangap, sangap,
	   Sangap Goar i.

5.	 Sudena na porsea i, 
	   Marolopolop be,
	   Di surgo di habangsa i,
	   Marsomba, somba, somba,
	   Marsomba sude. Amen.
