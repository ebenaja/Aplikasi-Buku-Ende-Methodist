1. 	O Jesus naeng tardidi
	    Do posoposo on.
	    Sai ro Ho mangaligi,
	    Huhut manjalo on.

2.	 Diboan natorasna,
	   Nasida be tuson,
	   Ai naeng pasahathonna,
	   Tu Ho dakdanak on.

3. O Jesus Tuhannami,
	   Silehon tua i,
	   MudarMu do naung durus,
	   Manesa dosa i.

4.	 Sai tatap ale Tuhan,
	   Nasida sasude,
	   Asa tung saut martua,
	   Rap sonang sasude.

5.	 Ho Ama, Anak, Tondi,
	   Urasi ma sude,
	   Huhut tongtong ramoti,
	   Nuaeng nang sogot pe. Amen.
