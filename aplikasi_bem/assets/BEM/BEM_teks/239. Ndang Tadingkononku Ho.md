	   Ale Jesus, Sipangolu
	   Naeng ihuthononku do,
	   Ho saleleng au mangolu.
	   Ho do hangoluanki,

2.	 Nasa na nilehonMi
	   Tondi ro di pamatangku,

	   Hupasahat i tu Ho,
	   Na so unsatonku do.

3. Nang lam buruk dagingki,

	   Majal pangkilalaanki,
	   Dila so malo marhata,
	   Tanda ajalhi naeng ro 
	   Jesus tioponku do. Amen.
